## Fundamental Analysis

Context from Microsoft’s FY2025 filing reference shows **revenue up ~+15% to ~$281.7B**, with growth across segments.

Valuation is a trade-off: **trailing P/E ~23.5x**, **forward P/E ~20.0x**, **P/S ~9.15x**, and **P/B ~7.15x**. While these reflect a premium quality multiple, the forward P/E below trailing suggests the market expects continued earnings progress rather than imminent multiple compression.

The clearest fundamental pressure is **margin dynamics from AI infrastructure scaling**. The filing context notes **Microsoft Cloud gross margin percentage decreasing to ~69%**, attributed to scaling AI infrastructure impacts, partially offset by Azure efficiency, alongside higher operating expenses as Microsoft invests in cloud/AI engineering.

**Fundamental takeaway:** overall **bullish**, but the case is sensitive to whether AI-related margin pressure is temporary versus persistent.
