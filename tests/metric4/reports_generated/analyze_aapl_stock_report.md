## Risk Factors
The risk agent rates AAPL **high** risk, driven by:
- **Operational/supply chain:** globally distributed manufacturing and limited/custom components; exposure to shortages and cost increases.
- **Regulatory:** privacy and online-safety compliance complexity; potential fines/liability.

**Mitigants:** supplier diversification for many components, encryption/security controls (not absolute), planning reserves and impairment/inventory reviews, and scale/international operations.
