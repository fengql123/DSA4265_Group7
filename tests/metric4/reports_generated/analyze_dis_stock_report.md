## Executive Summary

**Thesis:** Disney shows constructive fundamentals and a reasonable valuation backdrop, while near-term market timing is pressured by bearish technical momentum and meaningful execution/contingent risks.

- **Valuation:** Multiples are described as moderate: **P/E ~14–15x**, **P/S ~1.8x**, **P/B ~1.62x**.

**Bottom line:** Fundamental signals point **bullish**, but **soft earnings momentum** and **high leverage** reduce upside conviction in the near term.
